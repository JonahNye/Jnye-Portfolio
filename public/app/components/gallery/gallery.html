Trivia App

Enjoy a special effects laden trivia app designed to simulate everyone's favorite kind of trivia: bar night trivia!

This project uses JavaScript to dynamically scale the intensity of special CSS effects as the rounds progress and you finish your "drinks".

Two different APIs for trivia questions and beer types are accesssed to keep things interesting.

Please give this project time to load upon clicking on the screenshot to play!

Made with help from Brewery DB and Open Trivia DB APIs.
